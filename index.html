About me

Welcome to my website. My name is Natalia, I am 36 years old, I live in South Wales and I have been painting for almost 30 years, and my main inspiration is the art nouveau style and cats. I am a literary scholar by education, and I learned painting mainly by myself. I put 100% attention and maximum passion and dedication into each of my paintings. I love to focus on details, which gives my work a unique charm. I make paintings to order, I invite you to view my gallery and contact me.
